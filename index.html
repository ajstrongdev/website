About me

Hiya! I'm AJ! Welcome to my little home on the internet, where I like to show off the things I create. I'm a software engineer, freelance website developer and activist.

I first started learning website development in 2017 from school and fell in love with programming, from there I began to teach myself as much as I could. I was introduced to GNU/Linux and open source software in August 2020 and swapped from Windows 10 to Linux full-time in October 2020. I fell in love with open source software and the philosophy behind it.

I started doing freelance website development in 2021 after finding passion and enjoyment of website development. I love to learn new skills and try out new frameworks, and enjoy keeping up to date on the latest website trends.
